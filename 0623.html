Question 19
中文
芯片是指封装好的、包含完整电路的半导体微小基片（通常是硅片），广泛应用于电子设备。 近几年我国一直在芯片的工艺上进行技术攻坚，其中某芯片内核面积仅有 74.13 mm²，集成了约 69 亿个晶体管， 平均每个晶体管占有面积约为多少平方毫米？ （结果用科学记数法表示，保留两位小数。）
English
A chip is a packaged tiny semiconductor substrate, usually made of silicon, that contains a complete circuit and is widely used in electronic devices. In recent years, China has been making technical advances in chip manufacturing. One chip has a core area of only 74.13 mm² and integrates about 6.9 billion transistors. What is the average area occupied by each transistor in square millimetres? Express the answer in scientific notation and keep two decimal places.


Model Answer:
Number of transistors: 69 亿 = 6.9 × 10⁹

Average area per transistor:
74.13 ÷ (6.9 × 10⁹)
= 10.743478... × 10⁻⁹
= 1.0743478... × 10⁻⁸

Therefore, the average area is approximately:
1.07 × 10⁻⁸ mm²
Question 20
中文
地球表面平均 1 cm² 上的空气质量约为 1 kg， 地球表面的面积大约是 5 × 10⁸ km²， 地球表面全部空气的质量约为多少千克？ 已知地球的质量约为 6 × 10²⁴ kg， 它的质量大约是地球表面全部空气质量的多少倍？
English
The mass of air above an average area of 1 cm² on Earth’s surface is about 1 kg. The total surface area of Earth is about 5 × 10⁸ km². What is the approximate total mass of all the air above Earth’s surface in kilograms? Given that Earth’s mass is about 6 × 10²⁴ kg, about how many times larger is Earth’s mass than the total mass of the air above its surface?


Model Answer:
First convert square kilometres to square centimetres:
1 km = 10⁵ cm
1 km² = 10¹⁰ cm²

Earth’s surface area:
5 × 10⁸ km² = 5 × 10⁸ × 10¹⁰ cm²
= 5 × 10¹⁸ cm²

Since each 1 cm² has about 1 kg of air above it:
Total air mass = 5 × 10¹⁸ kg

Compare Earth’s mass with the air mass:
(6 × 10²⁴) ÷ (5 × 10¹⁸)
= 1.2 × 10⁶

Therefore:
The total mass of air is about 5 × 10¹⁸ kg.
Earth’s mass is about 1.2 × 10⁶ times the total air mass.
