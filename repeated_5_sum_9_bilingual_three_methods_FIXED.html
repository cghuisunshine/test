连续 5 的数列求和 Sum of Repeated 5s

一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：最后一项由 9 个 5 组成。 Original image: The last term consists of 9 digits, all 5.

计算：Calculate:

5 + 55 + 555 + ... + 555555555 （最后一项有 9 个 5）

二、解法一：把每一项写成 5×111…12. Method 1: Write Each Term as 5 × 111…1

第 k 项是 k 个 5，可以写成：The kth term has k digits, all 5, so it can be written as:

第 k 项 = 5 × (111...1) = 5 × (10k - 1) / 9

所以原式为：Therefore the original sum is:

S = Σ[k=1 到 9] 5(10k - 1)/9

提出 5/9：Factor out 5/9:

S = 5/9 × [(10 + 102 + ... + 109) - 9]

其中：Here:

10 + 102 + ... + 109 = (1010 - 10) / 9

代入：Substitute:

S = 5/9 × [(1010 - 10)/9 - 9] = 5(1010 - 10 - 81) / 81 = 5(1010 - 91) / 81 = 617283945
答案：617283945Answer: 617283945

三、解法二：竖式找规律3. Method 2: Column Pattern

把这些数竖着排列：Arrange the numbers vertically:

5 55 555 5555 55555 555555 5555555 55555555 555555555

从右往左看，第 1 列有 9 个 5，第 2 列有 8 个 5，……，第 9 列有 1 个 5。依次进位即可得到：From right to left, the first column has 9 fives, the second column has 8 fives, and so on. Carrying column by column gives:

617283945

四、解法三：观察规律 + 乘 5 等于乘 10 再除以 24. Method 3: Observe the Pattern + Multiply by 10, Then Divide by 2

补充图：先提出 5，再观察 1+11+111+… 的规律，最后把乘 5 看成乘 10 再除以 2。 Supplementary image: First factor out 5, then observe the pattern of 1+11+111+…, and finally treat multiplying by 5 as multiplying by 10 and dividing by 2.

先把每一项都提出 5：First factor out 5 from every term:

5 + 55 + 555 + ... + 555555555 = 5×1 + 5×11 + 5×111 + ... + 5×111111111 = 5×(1 + 11 + 111 + ... + 111111111)

观察括号里的和：Observe the sum inside the parentheses:

1 = 1 1 + 11 = 12 1 + 11 + 111 = 123 1 + 11 + 111 + 1111 = 1234

继续下去，可以发现规律：Continuing this pattern, we discover:

1 + 11 + 111 + ... + 111111111 = 123456789

所以原式变成：So the original sum becomes:

5 × 123456789

再用“乘 5 = 乘 10 再除以 2”：Now use “multiply by 5 = multiply by 10, then divide by 2”:

5 × 123456789 = 123456789 × 10 ÷ 2 = 1234567890 ÷ 2 = 617283945

最后一步可以用分段除法口算完成。The final step can be done mentally by dividing in parts.

答案：617283945Answer: 617283945
