一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：用简便方法计算。Original image: Calculate using a shortcut.

计算：Calculate:

25 × (785 + 783 + 786 + 782)

二、方法一：找平均数2. Method 1: Use the Average

观察括号里的 4 个数：Look at the 4 numbers inside the parentheses:

785, 783, 786, 782

它们都在 784 附近：They are all close to 784:

785 = 784 + 1 783 = 784 - 1 786 = 784 + 2 782 = 784 - 2

多出来的和少掉的正好抵消：The extra amounts and missing amounts cancel out:

(+1) + (-1) + (+2) + (-2) = 0

所以括号内的和等于：So the sum inside the parentheses is:

784 × 4

原式变成：The original expression becomes:

25 × (784 × 4)

因为 25 × 4 = 100，所以：Since 25 × 4 = 100:

25 × 784 × 4 = 784 × 100 = 78400
答案：78400Answer: 78400

三、方法二：配对相加3. Method 2: Pair the Numbers

把括号里的数配对：Pair the numbers inside the parentheses:

(785 + 783) + (786 + 782)

两组的和相同：Both pair sums are the same:

785 + 783 = 1568 786 + 782 = 1568

所以括号内的和是：So the sum inside the parentheses is:

1568 + 1568 = 3136

再乘以 25：Now multiply by 25:

25 × 3136

把 25 看成 100 ÷ 4：Think of 25 as 100 ÷ 4:

25 × 3136 = 3136 ÷ 4 × 100 = 784 × 100 = 78400
答案：78400Answer: 78400

四、方法三：按位拆分4. Method 3: Break into Hundreds, Tens, and Ones

辅助图：把每个数拆成 700、80 和个位部分。 Visual aid: Break each number into 700, 80, and the ones part.

把括号里的每个数按位拆开： Break each number inside the parentheses into place values:

785 = 700 + 80 + 5 783 = 700 + 80 + 3 786 = 700 + 80 + 6 782 = 700 + 80 + 2

所以括号里的和可以写成： So the sum inside the parentheses can be written as:

(700 + 80 + 5) + (700 + 80 + 3) + (700 + 80 + 6) + (700 + 80 + 2)

把相同数位合并： Combine the same place values:

= 700 × 4 + 80 × 4 + (5 + 3 + 6 + 2)

个位数相加： Add the ones digits:

5 + 3 + 6 + 2 = 16

所以括号里的和是： So the sum inside the parentheses is:

700 × 4 + 80 × 4 + 16 = 2800 + 320 + 16 = 3136

再乘以 25： Now multiply by 25:

25 × 3136 = 3136 ÷ 4 × 100 = 784 × 100 = 78400
答案：78400 Answer: 78400

25 × (785 + 783 + 786 + 782) = 78400
