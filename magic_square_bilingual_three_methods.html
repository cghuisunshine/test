一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：填上合适的数，使每行、每列、每条对角线三数之和相等。 Original image: Fill in numbers so that every row, column, and diagonal has the same sum.

已知：第一行中间是 9，第一行右边是 4，第二行右边是 3。Given: The top-middle is 9, the top-right is 4, and the middle-right is 3.

要求：填完整个 3×3 方格，使每行、每列、每条对角线的和都相等。Task: Complete the 3×3 grid so every row, column, and diagonal has the same sum.

二、解法一：利用 1~9 三阶幻方性质2. Method 1: Use the 1–9 Magic Square Property

如果用 1 到 9 填三阶幻方，那么九个数的总和是 45。If the square uses the numbers 1 to 9, the total sum is 45.

1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 8 + 9 = 45 45 ÷ 3 = 15

所以每行、每列、每条对角线的和都是 15。So each row, column, and diagonal must sum to 15.

□ + 9 + 4 = 15 → 左上角 = 2 4 + 3 + □ = 15 → 右下角 = 8 2 + □ + 8 = 15 → 中间 = 5 □ + 5 + 3 = 15 → 中左 = 7 2 + 7 + □ = 15 → 左下 = 6 6 + □ + 8 = 15 → 下中 = 1
按此可填完整个幻方。This completes the magic square.

三、解法二：设公和为 S3. Method 2: Let the Common Sum Be S

设左上角为 a，每行每列每条对角线的和为 S。Let the top-left number be a, and let the common sum be S.

第一行：a + 9 + 4 = S，所以 S = a + 13 第三列：4 + 3 + i = S，所以 i = a + 6 主对角线：a + e + i = S，所以 e = 7 - a 第二行：d + e + 3 = S，所以 d = 2a + 3 另一条对角线：4 + e + g = S，所以 g = 2a + 2

再用第一列：Now use the first column:

a + d + g = S a + (2a + 3) + (2a + 2) = a + 13 4a = 8 a = 2

于是 S = 15，其余各格就能继续求出。So S = 15, and the remaining cells can then be found.

得到同样答案。This gives the same final square.

四、解法三：位置关系推导4. Method 3: Derive a Position Relationship

补充图：通过“竖、斜、横”三条和与总和比较，可推出角上的数与相邻两个边中点的关系。 Supplementary image: Compare one column, one diagonal, and one row with the total sum to derive a relationship between a corner and two edge-middle cells.

如图，把九个格记作 a,b,c / d,e,f / g,h,i，公和记作 S。As shown, label the cells a,b,c / d,e,f / g,h,i and let the common sum be S.

竖：a + d + g = S 斜：a + e + i = S 横：a + b + c = S

把上面三式相加，再减去九个格子的总和 3S，可得：Add the three equations above, then subtract the total of all nine cells, which is also 3S. We get:

2a = h + f 所以 a = (h + f) / 2

也就是说：左上角 = “中右”和“下中”的平均数。So the top-left number equals the average of the middle-right and bottom-middle cells.

本题里，中右是 3。又因为幻方中心是 5，所以第二列有：In this puzzle, the middle-right cell is 3. Since the center is 5, the second column gives:

9 + 5 + h = 15 h = 1

于是：Therefore:

a = (h + f) / 2 = (1 + 3) / 2 = 2

再按行列继续填，就能得到完整答案。Then continue filling by rows and columns to obtain the full square.

这是一种很有用的幻方位置关系。This is a very useful magic-square position relationship.
