圆圈代表几？What Number Does the Circle Represent?

一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：圆圈代表同一个数。 Original image: The circles represent the same number.

已知：Given:

○ ÷ 4 = ○ - 24

求：○ 代表多少？Find: the value of ○.

二、方法一：设未知数2. Method 1: Use a Variable

设圆圈代表的数是 x。Let the circle represent x.

x ÷ 4 = x - 24

也就是：This is:

x / 4 = x - 24

两边同时乘以 4：Multiply both sides by 4:

x = 4x - 96

移项：Move terms:

96 = 3x

所以：So:

x = 32
○ = 32○ = 32

三、方法二：理解差值3. Method 2: Understand the Difference

辅助理解图：把圆圈平均分成 4 份，每份是 8，所以整个圆圈是 32。 Visual aid: The circle is split into 4 equal parts. Each part is 8, so the whole circle is 32.

题目说：The equation says:

○ ÷ 4 = ○ - 24

意思是：圆圈的四分之一，比圆圈本身少 24。 This means: one quarter of the circle number is 24 less than the whole circle number.

所以，圆圈本身减去圆圈的四分之一，等于 24： So the whole number minus one quarter of it equals 24:

○ - ○ ÷ 4 = 24

也就是：That is:

3/4 个 ○ = 24

那么 1/4 个 ○ 是：Then one quarter of ○ is:

24 ÷ 3 = 8

所以整个 ○ 是：So the whole ○ is:

8 × 4 = 32
○ = 32○ = 32

四、验算4. Check

把 ○ = 32 代入原式：Substitute ○ = 32 into the original equation:

32 ÷ 4 = 8 32 - 24 = 8

左右两边相等，所以答案正确。Both sides are equal, so the answer is correct.

最终答案：○ = 32Final answer: ○ = 32
