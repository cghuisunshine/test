一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：计算分数和。Original image: Calculate the sum of the fractions.

计算：Calculate:

1/2 + 1/4 + 1/8 + 1/16 + 1/32 = ?

二、方法一：通分2. Method 1: Common Denominator

分母 2、4、8、16、32 的最小公分母是 32。The least common denominator of 2, 4, 8, 16, and 32 is 32.

1/2 = 16/32 1/4 = 8/32 1/8 = 4/32 1/16 = 2/32 1/32 = 1/32

所以：So:

1/2 + 1/4 + 1/8 + 1/16 + 1/32 = (16 + 8 + 4 + 2 + 1) / 32 = 31 / 32
答案：31/32Answer: 31/32

三、方法二：看成“差一点到 1”3. Method 2: Think of It as “Almost 1”

连续相加，会越来越接近 1：As we add each term, the sum gets closer to 1:

1/2 + 1/4 = 3/4 3/4 + 1/8 = 7/8 7/8 + 1/16 = 15/16 15/16 + 1/32 = 31/32

最后只差 1/32 到 1，所以：At the end it is short of 1 by 1/32, so:

1 - 1/32 = 31/32
答案：31/32Answer: 31/32

四、方法三：等比数列规律4. Method 3: Geometric Pattern

这组数每一项都是前一项的一半：Each term is half of the previous term:

1/2, 1/4, 1/8, 1/16, 1/32

前 n 项规律是：The pattern for the first n terms is:

1/2 + 1/4 + ... + 1/(2^n) = 1 - 1/(2^n)

这里 n = 5：Here n = 5:

1 - 1/(2^5) = 1 - 1/32 = 31/32
答案：31/32Answer: 31/32

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

1/2 + 1/4 + 1/8 + 1/16 + 1/32 = 31/32
最终答案：31/32Final answer: 31/32
