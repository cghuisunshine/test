SLIDE 8 — COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

So where do we fit in against the competition?

Amazon and Newegg are strong on price and selection, but they can’t offer hands-on testing. You still can’t feel a mouse or try a keyboard before buying online.

Best Buy and Canada Computers are the closest physical competitors, but they focus on mainstream brands. They don’t carry enthusiast products like Wooting, Lamzu, or Artisan, and their staff usually aren’t community specialists.

That’s where RigZone stands out. We combine niche enthusiast products, in-store demos, expert gamer staff, community-driven curation, easy local returns, and competitive pricing — all in one place.

Our real advantage is the in-person testing experience backed by community trust. Even if online retailers expand their niche inventory, they still can’t replicate local demos, real conversations, and a store built around enthusiasts.
