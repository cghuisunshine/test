SLIDE 8 — COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

Here’s the competitive case.

Amazon and Newegg win on selection and price, but they cannot offer hands-on testing before purchase.

Best Buy and Canada Computers are the closest physical competitors, but they focus on mainstream brands. They do not carry enthusiast products like Wooting, Lamzu, or Artisan, and their staff are generalists rather than community specialists.

RigZone is the only retailer here that combines all six strengths: niche enthusiast products, in-store demos, expert gamer staff, community-driven curation, easy local returns, and competitive pricing.

Our moat is the in-store testing experience, strengthened by community trust. Even if online retailers add more niche SKUs, they still cannot replicate in-person demos and real local community relationships.
