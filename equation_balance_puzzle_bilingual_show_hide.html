一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：把数字填入圆圈内，使等式成立。 Original image: Put the numbers into the circles so that the equation is true.

已知数字：2，6，11，15，35，77 Given numbers: 2, 6, 11, 15, 35, 77

○ × ○ × ○ = ○ × ○ × ○

要求：把这 6 个数分别填入 6 个圆圈中，每个数用一次，使左右两边乘积相等。 Task: Place the 6 numbers into the 6 circles, using each number once, so that the products on the two sides are equal.

二、方法一：看总乘积的一半2. Method 1: Look at Half of the Total Product

先把 6 个数全部乘起来： First multiply all 6 numbers together:

2 × 6 × 11 × 15 × 35 × 77

把每个数做质因数分解： Prime factorize each number:

2 = 2 6 = 2 × 3 11 = 11 15 = 3 × 5 35 = 5 × 7 77 = 7 × 11

所以总乘积是： So the total product is:

(2) × (2 × 3) × (11) × (3 × 5) × (5 × 7) × (7 × 11) = 2² × 3² × 5² × 7² × 11² = (2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11)²

这说明左右两边如果相等，那么每一边都应该等于： This means that if the two sides are equal, then each side must equal:

2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11 = 2310

现在找 3 个数乘起来等于 2310： Now find 3 numbers whose product is 2310:

2 × 15 × 77 = 2 × (3 × 5) × (7 × 11) = 2310

剩下的 3 个数自然也是： The remaining 3 numbers are then:

6 × 11 × 35 = (2 × 3) × 11 × (5 × 7) = 2310
一种填法：2，15，77 | 6，11，35 One arrangement: 2, 15, 77 | 6, 11, 35

三、方法二：配对补全质因数3. Method 2: Complete the Prime Factors by Grouping

观察这些数字的结构： Notice the structure of these numbers:

2 6 = 2 × 3 11 15 = 3 × 5 35 = 5 × 7 77 = 7 × 11

它们像一条链： They form a chain:

2 → 3 → 5 → 7 → 11

为了让两边相等，每边最好都凑出同样的一组质因数： To make both sides equal, each side should contain the same full set of prime factors:

2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11

一边可以取： One side can take:

2，15，77

因为： Because:

2 × 15 × 77 = 2 × (3 × 5) × (7 × 11) = 2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11

另一边取剩下的： The other side uses the remaining numbers:

6，11，35

因为： Because:

6 × 11 × 35 = (2 × 3) × 11 × (5 × 7) = 2 × 3 × 5 × 7 × 11
另一种写法：6，11，35 | 2，15，77 Equivalent arrangement: 6, 11, 35 | 2, 15, 77

四、验算4. Check

左边：2 × 15 × 77 = 2310 右边：6 × 11 × 35 = 2310

左右两边相等，所以填法正确。 The two sides are equal, so the arrangement is correct.

最终答案：2 × 15 × 77 = 6 × 11 × 35 Final answer: 2 × 15 × 77 = 6 × 11 × 35
