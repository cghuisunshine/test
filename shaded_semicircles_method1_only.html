一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：AB = 10，BC = 20，求阴影部分面积。 Original image: AB = 10 and BC = 20. Find the shaded area.

题意：直角三角形 ABC 中，AB = 10，BC = 20。在 AB 和 BC 上分别作半圆，阴影部分是两个半圆合起来减去直角三角形内部的部分。 Meaning: In right triangle ABC, AB = 10 and BC = 20. Semicircles are drawn on AB and BC. The shaded area equals the two semicircles minus the right triangle area.

二、解法一：分别求两个半圆2. Method 1: Find the Two Semicircles Separately

阴影面积 = 半圆 AB 面积 + 半圆 BC 面积 − 直角三角形 ABC 面积。 Shaded area = area of semicircle on AB + area of semicircle on BC − area of right triangle ABC.

AB = 10，所以半径是 5：AB = 10, so the radius is 5:

S₁ = 1/2 × π × 5² = 25π / 2

BC = 20，所以半径是 10：BC = 20, so the radius is 10:

S₂ = 1/2 × π × 10² = 50π

直角三角形面积：Area of the right triangle:

S△ABC = 1/2 × 10 × 20 = 100

所以：Therefore:

S阴影 = 25π/2 + 50π - 100 = 125π/2 - 100
阴影部分面积 = 125π/2 − 100Shaded area = 125π/2 − 100

三、最终答案3. Final Answer

S阴影 = 125π/2 - 100
最终答案：125π/2 − 100，约 96.25Final answer: 125π/2 − 100, about 96.25
