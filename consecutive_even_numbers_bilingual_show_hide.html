一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：填入 4 个连续的偶数。 Original image: Fill in 4 consecutive even numbers.

题目：填入 4 个连续的偶数，使它们的乘积等于 13440。 Problem: Fill in 4 consecutive even numbers so that their product is 13440.

□ × □ × □ × □ = 13440

二、方法一：从平方附近试2. Method 1: Try Numbers Near the Middle

4 个连续偶数可以写成： Four consecutive even numbers can be written as:

n, n + 2, n + 4, n + 6

因为 13440 大约接近： Since 13440 is close to:

10 × 10 × 10 × 10 = 10000

所以这 4 个偶数应该在 10 附近。试连续偶数： So the four even numbers should be near 10. Try the consecutive even numbers:

8, 10, 12, 14

验算乘积： Check the product:

8 × 10 × 12 × 14 = 80 × 168 = 13440
答案：8，10，12，14 Answer: 8, 10, 12, 14

三、方法二：质因数分解3. Method 2: Prime Factorization

先把 13440 分解： First factor 13440:

13440 = 1344 × 10 = 2⁶ × 3 × 5 × 7

如果答案是 8、10、12、14，那么： If the answer is 8, 10, 12, 14, then:

8 = 2³ 10 = 2 × 5 12 = 2² × 3 14 = 2 × 7

把它们相乘： Multiplying them gives:

8 × 10 × 12 × 14 = 2³ × (2 × 5) × (2² × 3) × (2 × 7) = 2⁷ × 3 × 5 × 7 = 13440

所以这组连续偶数正确。 So this set of consecutive even numbers is correct.

四、最终答案4. Final Answer

8 × 10 × 12 × 14 = 13440
4 个连续偶数是：8，10，12，14 The 4 consecutive even numbers are: 8, 10, 12, 14
