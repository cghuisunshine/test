1. planning-with-files

Pain point: 做项目做到一半忘了全局 — You get halfway through a project and lose the big picture.

What it is

A workflow skill for planning while directly using your files, notes, docs, specs, and codebase as context. It helps you keep the overall structure of the project in mind instead of only focusing on the current small task.

How to use it
  • Give it your project files, requirements, notes, or drafts.
  • Ask it to summarize the overall project goal.
  • Ask it to break the work into milestones, tasks, and dependencies.
