一、题目 1. Problem

题目原图：求阴影部分面积。 Original image: Find the area of the shaded region.

题意：图中是一个直角三角形，左下角有一个阴影长方形，长方形的右上角落在三角形的斜边上。 Meaning: The figure is a right triangle. A shaded rectangle is placed at the lower left, and its upper-right corner lies on the hypotenuse.

  • 长方形上方左边剩余的高为 2The remaining height above the rectangle on the left is 2.
  • 长方形右边剩余的底边为 10The remaining base to the right of the rectangle is 10.

求：阴影部分的面积。 Find: the area of the shaded rectangle.

二、解法一：设未知数 + 相似关系 2. Method 1: Variables + Similar Triangles

设阴影长方形的宽为 x，高为 y。 Let the shaded rectangle have width x and height y.

  • 阴影面积：S = xyShaded area: S = xy
  • 大三角形的总高：y + 2Total height of the large triangle: y + 2
  • 大三角形的总底：x + 10Total base of the large triangle: x + 10

由于右边的小三角形与整个大三角形相似，所以对应边成比例： The small right triangle is similar to the whole large triangle, so corresponding sides are proportional:

y / (y + 2) = 10 / (x + 10)

交叉相乘：Cross multiply:

y(x + 10) = 10(y + 2)

展开：Expand:

xy + 10y = 10y + 20

两边同时减去 10y：Subtract 10y from both sides:

xy = 20

而阴影面积正好就是 xy，所以： The shaded area is exactly xy, so:

阴影部分面积 = 20 平方单位Shaded area = 20 square units

三、解法二：割补法（补成矩形） 3. Method 2: Cut-and-Fill, Complete the Rectangle

补图：把原图补成一个大矩形。 Completed diagram: The original figure is extended into a large rectangle.

把原图补齐后，可以得到一个大矩形。观察可知： After completing the figure, we get a large rectangle. Notice that:

  • 上方补出来的那一条阴影带，高就是左边标出的 2The height of the added shaded strip on top is the left marked length, 2.
  • 从中间竖线到右边底角的长度是 10The length from the middle vertical line to the right bottom corner is 10.

所以右上方补出来的阴影长方形面积是：So the area of the added upper-right shaded rectangle is:

10 × 2 = 20

再看从左上角到右下角的斜线，它是这个大矩形的一条对角线。 Now look at the slanted line from the upper-left corner to the lower-right corner. It is a diagonal of the large rectangle.

而矩形的对角线会把矩形分成面积相等的两部分。 A diagonal of a rectangle divides it into two equal-area parts.

因此，上方补出来的阴影面积等于下方对应部分的面积，也就是等于原来左下角的阴影长方形面积。 Therefore, the added shaded area above equals the corresponding shaded area below, which is the original lower-left shaded rectangle.

所以原阴影面积也是：So the original shaded area is also:

阴影部分面积 = 20 平方单位Shaded area = 20 square units

四、总结 4. Summary

这道题可以用两种思路来做：There are two good ways to solve this problem:

  1. 相似法：设长方形长和宽为未知数，通过相似三角形列比例，直接得到 xy = 20。Similar-triangle method: Use variables for the rectangle and set up a proportion to get xy = 20.
  2. 割补法：把图补成矩形，利用“矩形对角线平分面积”的性质，得到阴影面积等于补出的 10 × 2。Cut-and-fill method: Complete the figure into a rectangle and use the fact that a rectangle’s diagonal halves its area, giving the shaded area as 10 × 2.
最终答案：20 平方单位Final answer: 20 square units
