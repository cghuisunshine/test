一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：在圆圈里填入 ＞、＜ 或 ＝。 Original image: Fill the circle with ＞, ＜, or ＝.

比较：Compare:

1.005²⁰⁰ ○ 2

二、解法一：用展开中的正项判断2. Solution 1: Use the Positive Terms in Expansion

把 1.005 写成：Write 1.005 as:

1.005 = 1 + 0.005

所以：So:

1.005²⁰⁰ = (1 + 0.005)²⁰⁰

展开时，先看前两部分：When expanding, first look at the first two parts:

(1 + 0.005)²⁰⁰ = 1 + 200 × 0.005 + 其他正项

因为：Because:

200 × 0.005 = 1

所以：Therefore:

(1 + 0.005)²⁰⁰ = 1 + 1 + 其他正项 = 2 + 其他正项 > 2
应填：＞Fill in: ＞

三、解法二：用“不低于一次增长”的思想3. Solution 2: Use the “At Least First Growth” Idea

1.005 比 1 多 0.005。乘 200 次时，最基本的一次增长量已经达到：1.005 is 0.005 more than 1. Over 200 factors, the basic first-order growth already reaches:

200 × 0.005 = 1

仅考虑这个增长，就有：Considering only this growth gives:

1 + 200 × 0.005 = 2

实际乘方还包含更多正的乘积项，所以一定更大：The actual power also includes more positive product terms, so it must be greater:

1.005²⁰⁰ > 2
应填：＞Fill in: ＞

四、解法三：连乘缩项法（高斯思路）4. Solution 3: Telescoping Product Method

解法三配图：把 1.005²⁰⁰ 与一串可缩项的连乘积比较。 Solution 3 illustration: Compare 1.005²⁰⁰ with a telescoping product.

先把 1.005 写成：First write 1.005 as:

1.005 = 1 + 0.005 = 1 + 1/200

所以：So:

1.005²⁰⁰ = (1 + 1/200)²⁰⁰

因为Because

1 + 1/200 > 1 + 1/201 > 1 + 1/202 > ... > 1 + 1/399

于是 200 个相同因子的乘积，大于下面这一串 200 个逐渐变小的因子的乘积：the product of 200 equal factors is greater than the following product of 200 gradually smaller factors:

(1 + 1/200)²⁰⁰ > (1 + 1/200)(1 + 1/201)(1 + 1/202)...(1 + 1/399)

把每个括号化成分数：Convert each factor into a fraction:

(1 + 1/200)(1 + 1/201)...(1 + 1/399) = 201/200 × 202/201 × 203/202 × ... × 400/399

这是一个连乘缩项，前后约掉后只剩：This is a telescoping product. After cancellation, only the first denominator and last numerator remain:

201/200 × 202/201 × 203/202 × ... × 400/399 = 400/200 = 2

所以：Therefore:

1.005²⁰⁰ > 2
应填：＞Fill in: ＞

五、最终答案5. Final Answer

1.005²⁰⁰ > 2
