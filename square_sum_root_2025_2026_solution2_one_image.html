一、题目1. Problem

题目原图：已知 2025² + 2026² = a，求 √(2a − 1)。 Original image: Given 2025 squared plus 2026 squared equals a, find the square root of 2a minus 1.

已知：Given:

2025² + 2026² = a

求：Find:

√(2a - 1)

二、解法一：代数法2. Solution 1: Algebra

关键是发现 2025 和 2026 是连续整数。设 2025 = n，则 2026 = n + 1。The key is to notice that 2025 and 2026 are consecutive integers. Let 2025 be n, so 2026 is n plus 1.

2025 = n 2026 = n + 1

由题意：From the problem:

a = n² + (n + 1)²

计算 2a - 1：Compute 2a minus 1:

2a - 1 = 2[n² + (n + 1)²] - 1 = 2[n² + n² + 2n + 1] - 1 = 4n² + 4n + 1 = (2n + 1)²

所以：Therefore:

√(2a - 1) = √[(2n + 1)²] = 2n + 1

代入 n = 2025：Substitute n equals 2025:

2n + 1 = 2 × 2025 + 1 = 4051
答案：4051Answer: 4051

三、解法二：几何拼图法3. Solution 2: Geometry / Dissection Method

这个方法把式子看成面积。下图中已经把 2026²、2025² 和中间的 1 拼成了一个大正方形。This method interprets the expression as area. In the figure below, the parts 2026 squared, 2025 squared, and the center 1 are arranged into one large square.

几何解法图：只用这一张图观察大正方形的边长与面积关系。 Geometry solution image: Use this single diagram to observe the side length and area relationship of the large square.
观察：大正方形的一条边由 2026 和 2025 两段组成，所以大正方形边长是 2026 + 2025。 Observation: One side of the large square is made of two segments, 2026 and 2025. So the side length of the large square is 2026 plus 2025.

因此，大正方形的面积是：Therefore, the area of the large square is:

(2026 + 2025)²

而图中所有小块拼起来，表示的正是：And all the pieces in the figure together represent:

2a - 1

所以有：So we have:

2a - 1 = (2026 + 2025)²

两边开方：Taking the square root of both sides:

√(2a - 1) = √[(2026 + 2025)²] = 2026 + 2025 = 4051
答案：4051Answer: 4051

四、最终答案4. Final Answer

√(2a - 1) = 4051
